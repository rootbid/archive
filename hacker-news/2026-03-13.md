**[ATMs didn’t kill bank teller jobs, but the iPhone did]**

- **Discussion:** The discussion draws parallels between historical automation (like ATMs and mechanized farming) and the current AI wave. HN users debate whether AI-driven productivity will create new types of employment or lead to a "K-shaped" economy characterized by extreme wealth concentration.
