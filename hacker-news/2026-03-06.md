# Hacker News Daily Summary - March 6, 2026

**[Judge orders government to begin refunding more than $130B in tariffs](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47261688)**
- **Summary:** A US federal judge has ordered the government to start refunding over $130 billion in tariffs collected during the previous administration's trade wars. This follows a long-running legal battle where businesses challenged the legality of the "List 3" and "List 4A" tariffs on Chinese imports.
- **Discussion:** [HN Discussion](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47261688). Users are debating the economic impact and the legal precedent for executive tariff authority. Key themes include the complexity of interest calculations and whether this will lower consumer prices.

**[GPT-5.4](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47265045)**

**[Wikipedia was in read-only mode following mass admin account compromise](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47263323)**

- **Discussion:** [HN Discussion](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47263323). Security analysis focuses on 2FA failures, session hijacking, and the risks of centralized trust in a decentralized contributor model.

**[Good software knows when to stop](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47261561)**

**[CBP tapped into the online advertising ecosystem to track peoples’ movements](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47249387)**

**[10% of Firefox crashes are caused by bitflips](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47252971)**

**[The Brand Age](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47264756)**
