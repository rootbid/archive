# Daily Market Sentiment Report: March 13, 2026

## Global Market Sentiment: Cautious & Negative
Global equities are facing significant headwinds driven primarily by geopolitical escalation and surging energy costs. 
* **Commodity Shock:** Brent crude has hit the $100 mark amid an ongoing US-Israel-Iran conflict, sending shockwaves through global markets.
* **Wall Street:** US markets (S&P 500 and Dow) plunged following the oil shock and higher-than-expected Producer Price Index (PPI) wholesale price growth.
* **Europe:** European indices (FTSE 100, DAX, CAC 40) are trading lower in the red, reflecting widespread risk-off sentiment.

## Indian Market Sentiment: Bearish (Oil Price Impact)
The Indian stock market experienced a severe "bloodbath" as global macroeconomic pressures weighed heavily on domestic indices.
* **Market Performance:** The Sensex tumbled over 800 points to close deep in the red, while the Nifty 50 settled lower by around 1% at 23,639. 
* **Sector Drags:** Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) and auto sectors led the market drag. 
* **Currency Impact:** The Indian Rupee tumbled to an all-time low against the US Dollar due to the oil shock.
* **Outlook:** Technical analysts warn that selling may intensify if Nifty breaks the crucial 23,500 support level, with VIX staying above 21 indicating high volatility. However, the resulting correction has narrowed India's premium valuation, making it potentially attractive for long-term investors.
